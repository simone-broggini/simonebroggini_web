About Me

My journey into product management is deeply rooted in a lifelong passion for audio. From my early days as an audio engineer to my current role building tools for creators, my focus has always been on the intersection of technology and creativity. I thrive on the challenge of understanding complex workflows and creating intuitive, impactful solutions that empower users.

My approach to product is centered on a deep understanding of the user, a commitment to cross-disciplinary collaboration, and a focus on building intuitive and impactful tools. I am a pragmatic product manager who prioritizes principles over rigid processes. I advocate for a design-first approach, emphasizing user experience and intuitive solutions, and I believe in rapid prototyping to test ideas and gather feedback efficiently.

You can find my detailed professional history on LinkedIn.

I also write about product management and creativity on my Substack, and I co-host the Da Grandi podcast.

I occasionally explore applied AI/ML; see selected projects on GitHub.
