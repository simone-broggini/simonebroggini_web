Case Studies

DAVID Systems - Web-based Scheduling Product

Context:

Owned the lifecycle of a web-based scheduling product used by European public broadcasters.

Role:

Product Manager. I drove the product from discovery through rollout, leading to 50% user growth in under 6 months. I also contributed to SDK development for hardware-software integration, enabling LAWO VisTool to control our product's playout components via a customisable interface.

Impact:

Led continuous discovery through user interviews, prototype tests, and validation experiments to inform roadmap decisions and rapidly test feature hypotheses that generated €100K in revenue. Managed a SCRUM team of 6 developers and cross-functional stakeholders, introducing KPIs for product performance and adoption, increasing visibility into feature success and user satisfaction.

Audible, Amazon - Internal CMS

Context:

Led user research with 20+ global stakeholders across departments to map workflows, identify pain points, and define requirements for a new internal CMS.

Role:

Product Specialist. I partnered with product managers and engineers to define user stories, implement UAT processes, and accelerate delivery of a new internal CMS.

Impact:

Reduced time-to-launch by 30%. Defined and executed a strategic release roadmap for a €1.5M budget, managing the production pipeline for 80-90 monthly titles on Audible.it. Redesigned cross-departmental communication protocols, resolving inter-team dependencies to reduce project delivery time by 50% and cut production costs by 15%.
